# Product

## Register

product

## Users
General users looking for a fast, clean, and friendly space to publish quick thoughts and browse others' updates without the clutter and noise of mainstream social media.

## Product Purpose
A minimalist public microblogging platform for sharing and discovering concise text updates. Success is a lightweight, easy-to-use interface that feels welcoming, interactive, and alive.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, inviting, and playful.
- **Three words**: Warm, Lively, Accessible
- **Emotional goals**: Connection, lightness, and delight

## Anti-references
- Busy, bloated social feeds overflowing with ads, sidebars, and infinite notifications.
- Cold, clinical developer-dashboard aesthetics (sharp corners, default monochrome shades, sterile forms).

## Design Principles
- **Welcoming Comfort**: Design with soft rounded corners, warm secondary highlights, and friendly typography to build an approachable environment.
- **Uncluttered Focus**: Focus purely on the content (the posts). Keep metadata minimal and the layout spacious and easy to read.
- **Playful Tactility**: Every primary action (like button click, post submission, tab switch) should offer satisfying, organic micro-interactions that make the app feel alive and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict adherence to WCAG AA color contrast (at least 4.5:1 for body and interaction states).
- Clear, soft focus rings for all interactive elements to support keyboard-only users.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` settings, replacing active transitions with immediate transitions or simple fades.
